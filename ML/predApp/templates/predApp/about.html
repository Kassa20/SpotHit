The model is trained to predict based on danceability, energy, and the other features listed above.

Each feature has a magnitude. To make the model more accurate, its better to use features with a higher magnitude.

  1. Danceability - Strength and regularity of the beat
  2. Energy - How fast and noisy the song sounds
  3. speechiness - How much spoken words are in a track
  4. acousticness - Likelyhood that the song uses acoustic instruments
  5. instrumentalness - Detects whether a track contains no vocals
  6. liveness - Detects whether the track was performed live
  7. valence - How cheerful the song sounds
